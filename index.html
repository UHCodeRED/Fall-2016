CodeRED is Houston's Premier Innovation Experience

Hosted by CougarCS, CodeRED is a place for students to have fun, learn something new, explore possibilities, solve problems, and build out their dreams without fear or doubt.

Whether you are building your first website, diving into the world of virtual reality, or building something truly game changing in an industry, come join us at CodeRED 2016. We'll provide you with everything you need - food, mentors, hardware, swag, etc. Just make sure you come hungry, curious, and eager to build your future.

Tracks

This CodeRED, we'll be introducing Tracks. Tracks offer a way for us to provide additional resources, incentives, and guidance by integrating specific themes into your projects. Tracks aren't required to be the core of a project, but they can serve as a way improve a project and one's skills.

FAQ

What is a hackathon?

Hackathons have taken the world by storm, and they're not about breaking or stealing. They're about solving problems, building ideas, and pushing the limits of the mind and body to create something amazing.

Where is CodeRED Curiosity?

It will be at the University of Houston Student Center South.

Who can attend?

Any undergraduate and graduate student from any college, university, or higher education institution. Graduates who graduated within 1 year of the event may also attend.

Can high schoolers attend?

High schoolers can attend! Do not hesitate to sign up.

How much does it cost?

Absolutely nothing for the event. We'll be providing snacks and meals, and sponsors will be handing out swag and prizes.

What do I need to bring?

A computer (preferably laptop), student ID, chargers, a sleeping bag if needed, and anything you need to maintain hygiene.

Travel Reimbursement?

We will be providing up to $200 travel reimbursement for approved applications. Travel reimbursement amount will be determined based on travel distance and will be distributed during the event. We are currently not accepting new reimbursement applications.

How big can a team be?

We suggest no more than five people, and please don't be a one man team. The real fun and productivity happens when you're a team. Note: We will only be providing prizes for up to four people per team.

How do I get there?

Drive, carpool, Uber/Lyft, airplane, bus, etc. We will be providing busses for high demand locations.

How can my area get a bus?

If we find that an area has a true high demand and funds are sufficient, we will provide a bus.

What if I don't have a team or idea?

Don't worry! That's pretty common, you'll have time before the hacking begins to find a team. We'll also have Facebook group for attendees.

Do I have to be a CS or Technology major?

Absolutely not! Any major may attend, and we frequently find many business, biology, and engineering majors at hackathons. All levels of skill and skillsets are welcome.

I would like to be a volunteer and/or mentor

Fantastic! We're excited that you would like to volunteer and/or mentor. Contact us at hackathon@cougarcs.com.

I want to attend, but I'm not sure if I can make it

Apply! If you get accepted we will ask for confirmation. If you confirm and cannot attend, please let us know ASAP so we can let someone else in.

What is your Code of Conduct?

CodeRED abides by the MLH code of conduct. For more info see here

Will CodeRED sponsor visas?

Unfortunately, due to legal complications, we will not be sponsoring visas for individuals wishing to come from other countries.
